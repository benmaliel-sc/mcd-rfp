AI Issue Capture — photo + voice

Snap a photo, dictate a sentence, AI populates the form. Manager logs the issue and returns to duties in seconds.

Shift mgrRestaurant mgrArea supervisor
TodayFree-text forms with mandatory fields. Manager either ignores it (peak shift), tells someone (forgotten), or scribbles a note (lost).
WhyThis is how SC delivers Paul's stated ask — "removing free-text fields, more automated smart fields." Photo + voice means no free text and no form-navigation friction. Makes it faster to log than to forget.
